About me

Front End Developer

Why did I get into this?

Developing websites became a passion of mine when I realized how small changes can make such a significant positive impact on the user experience, and improve access to information in both directions.
Developing websites is a way in which I believe I can positively impact many lives in a small but sustainable way. Using problem solving to create truly responsive websites, with consideration to accessibility entices users to continue to interact with the site.

What makes me an ideal member for your team?

Coming from a health professional background, I have excellent communication skills and work well as a part of a team. I also thrive working autonomously and have a good balance of being self-sufficent and asking for help when needed. I derive satisfaction from the end result so enjoy delivering the goods. I am passionate about ongoing learning and strive for continuous improvement.

Problem solving and puzzles are a passion of mine, including solving a dodecahedron rubix and bouldering (puzzles for the whole body).
